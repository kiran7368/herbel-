About Tulsi

Tulsi, also known as Holy Basil, is a sacred plant in India revered for its medicinal properties. It is widely used in Ayurveda for its ability to promote health and well-being.

Medicinal Uses

  • Boosts immunity and fights infections.
  • Reduces stress and promotes mental clarity.
  • Supports respiratory health and relieves cough.
  • Acts as a natural detoxifier and antioxidant.

Cultivation Methods

Tulsi thrives in warm climates and requires well-drained soil. It can be grown in pots or gardens with adequate sunlight and regular watering.
